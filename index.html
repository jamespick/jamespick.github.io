MUSIC

Pick Patek is an established rapper, singer, and producer based in Pittsburgh, PA. Patek's music is a unique blend of rap and pop music, taking inspiration from artists like Kanye West, A$AP Rocky, and Tyler, The Creator.

ABOUT

Pick Patek has been making music for three years, and already he has built a strong buzz locally in Pittsburgh and nationally in cities like Los Angeles, Chicago, and Dallas. In April, 2018, Patek released a 10 song mixtape "When In Oakland," which has amassed over 1 million streams to date. Stand out songs include Skywards, Off White, and Blue October.

In October, 2018, Patek released a 5 track EP called "Blue Slides & Night Skies" with lead single My Luck amassing over 100k streams to date.

Since December, 2018, Patek has been working with Ryan M. Tedder, a grammy nominated platinum producer, and Aaron Karsh, another producer from Pittsburgh on his newest project "Modern Art." The project is set to release on September, 26, 2019.

Patek has performed at Coda and Voltage Lounge, two venues in Philadelphia, opening for Dizzy Wright and Kap G respectively. He has also performed at Stage AE in Pittsburgh and at various smaller venues, showcases, and festivals. Patek recently hosted his own concert in a backyard (due to the lack of local venues in Pittsburgh) and had over 200 people in attendance.
