MUSIC

Pick Patek is an established rapper/vocalist based in Pittsburgh and Philadelphia. Pick's style is an alternative hip-hop, a blend between Chance the Rapper, Lido and Flume. Those artists, as well as artists like Vic Mensa, Joey Bada$$ and Domo Genesis are among Pick's influences.

ABOUT

Pick Patek has been making music his whole life, but he began to take rapping seriously when he was 16. After forming the hip-hop collective Huff, which consisted of a few rappers and producers, he released two solo mixtapes (After School Sessions in October, 2016 and Chune SZN in May, 2017) and one collaborative mixtape with Huff (The Huff Tape: Volume 1 in Jannuary, 2017). In July, 2017, Pick released his first music video for his single Off White, and since then, he's been working non-stop on his next, full length, LP.

Throughout that time, Pick has performed on several occasions at various clubs in Philadelphia, once opening for the 2017 XXL freshman Kap G. Additionally, he has hosted two backyard concerts, both of which brought in over 200 people.
